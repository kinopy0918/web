The View

海を、ひらく。Open to the sea.

大きな窓の先に、別府湾。朝の光も、夜の街灯りも、ぜんぶこの部屋のもの。Beppu Bay, just beyond the wide windows. Morning light and evening glow — all of it, yours from this room.

The House

時を、住む。Living with time.

革のソファ、流木のあかり。古いものと新しい心地よさが、静かに同居する。A leather sofa, a driftwood lamp. The old and the new, quietly at home together.

The Room

静けさを、敷く。A spread of stillness.

畳と障子、提灯のひかり。布団に身をあずければ、波の音だけが残る。Tatami, shoji, the glow of a paper lantern. Sink into the futon, and only the waves remain.

The Kitchen

暮らすように。As if you lived here.

緑のレトロキッチンで火を入れて、長いテーブルを囲む。それだけで、旅になる。Cook in the retro green kitchen, gather at the long table. That alone becomes a journey.

The Garden

春を、待つ。Waiting for spring.

桜と海が、同じ窓のなかに。季節がめぐるたび、庭の表情も変わる。Cherry blossoms and the sea, framed in a single window. With every season, the garden shows a new face.

Spring

ひかりの庭。Garden of light.

花のトンネルをくぐって、海へ降りていく。Through a tunnel of blossoms, down toward the sea.

Keep the horizon and the steaming onsen town to yourselves, and unwind as a family or group. Four semi-double beds (in two rooms) plus four single futons — a space with a warm, nostalgic Shōwa-retro feel.

Around · 別府八湯Around · Beppu Onsen

湯けむりの街を、めぐる。Wander the town of steam.

海庭を拠点に、別府八湯の湯巡りを。徒歩6分の東町温泉から、情緒あふれる竹瓦温泉、地獄めぐりで名高い鉄輪温泉まで、個性豊かな名湯が点在。ご宿泊の方には、地元目線の湯巡りガイドをご用意しています。朝湯・昼湯・夜湯、何度でも。Make uminiwa your base for Beppu's celebrated hot springs. From Higashimachi Onsen (6 minutes on foot) to the atmospheric Takegawara Onsen and Kannawa — home of the "Hells of Beppu" — distinctive baths lie all around. Guests receive a local's guide to the springs. Morning, noon, or night, bathe as often as you like.
